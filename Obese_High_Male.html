Obese - High Intensity Workouts (Male)

Deadlifts

Stand with your feet hip-width apart and a barbell in front of you. Bend your knees, push your hips back, and grasp the barbell with an overhand grip. Keep your back straight, lift the barbell by extending your hips and knees, and stand tall. Lower the barbell back down to the starting position. Perform 3 sets of 10 reps.

Squat jumps

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your body into a squat position and then explode upward, jumping as high as you can. Land softly back into the squat position and repeat. Perform 3 sets of 10 reps.

Burpees

Start in a standing position, then squat down and place your hands on the floor in front of you. Kick your feet back, so you are in a push-up position. Lower your chest to the ground, then push back up to the starting position. Jump your feet forward to your hands, then explode upward into a jump, reaching your arms overhead. Perform 3 sets of 10 reps.

Kettlebell swings

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a kettlebell with both hands between your legs. Bend your knees slightly, hinge at the hips, and swing the kettlebell back between your legs. Explosively extend your hips and swing the kettlebell up to shoulder height, keeping your arms straight. Control the swing back down between your legs and repeat. Perform 3 sets of 10 reps.

Box jumps

Stand facing a sturdy box or platform. Jump onto the box, landing with both feet, then step or jump back down to the starting position. Perform 3 sets of 10 reps.

Sledgehammer slams

Hold a sledgehammer with both hands, standing with your feet shoulder-width apart. Raise the sledgehammer overhead, then forcefully bring it down in front of you, as if you are slamming it into the ground. Alternate sides with each repetition. Perform 3 sets of 10 reps.

Mountain climbers

Start in a push-up position. Bring one knee toward your chest, then quickly switch and bring the other knee toward your chest. Continue alternating knees in a running motion. Perform for 30 seconds per set.

Battle rope waves

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding one end of a battle rope in each hand. Quickly raise and lower your arms, creating waves in the rope. Perform for 30 seconds per set.
